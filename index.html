Game rules:

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND score
  • There are two dices. The player looses his current score when one of them is a 1. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • Players can set the winning score in the middle green input box, so that you can change the predefined score of 100. (The default winning score is 100)
